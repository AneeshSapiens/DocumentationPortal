2. About Sapiens Decision Manager (DM)

Decision Manager (DM) is a business Decision management tool that enables you to sketch, validate, test, deploy, and manage business Decisions throughout their life cycle.

Sapiens Decision Manager (DM) is an innovative Decision management tool, based on the patented TDM (The Decision model) methodology and developed using state of the art HTML5 technology. It is a Single-Page Application (SPA), a web application that loads a single HTML page and dynamically updates that page as the user interacts with the application.

By concentrating company-wide business logic in a single repository, DM enables business users and managers to collaborate in understanding the business logic requirements and arrive at the necessary Decisions. The documented Decisions then become part of the system’s database and are maintained for the rest of the life cycle.

IT has resolved many of the major challenges facing business system development today and they can rapidly build even the most complex and scalable of systems. Advanced technologies, including business rule systems, have been great helpful at implementing business systems faster, saving time and money.

The remaining challenges are primarily on the business side: discovering, defining, validating, and managing the business policies, regulations, and operational Decisions – which we collectively refer to as business logic – that exist in and across business systems and then conveying the business logic requirements to IT in an efficient and unambiguous way.

Using the DM application, business users can implement the business logic requirements themselves in robust Decision models that can be automatically transformed into executable code, providing business and IT a common understanding, one that revolutionizes the development and implementation of business systems. DM implements the Decision models in a repository so robust that it enables the enterprise to discover, author, manage, and deploy its logic with minimal support from IT.

Creating Decision models in DM lowers the cost of development, speeds time to market, simplifies and improves automated and non–automated business processes, reducing operational cost while dramatically improving business governance, reducing risk in the enterprise, and enhancing compliance.
